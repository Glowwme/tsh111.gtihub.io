Balanced Nutrition Plan

Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado
  • Lunch: Grilled fish with salad
  • Dinner: Quinoa with mixed veggies
  • Snacks: Fresh fruits and a handful of nuts

Fitness Tips:

  • Regular physical activity (30 minutes a day)

  • Prioritize sleep and recovery

Specialized Diet Plan

Meal Plan:

  • For Diabetic: Low-carb meals with lean proteins
  • For Gluten-Free: Avoid wheat, barley, and rye
  • For Heart Health: Focus on omega-3s and low-sodium foods

Fitness Tips:

  • Consult with a doctor or nutritionist before starting
  • Tailor exercise intensity to individual capabilities
